*Please note, cat food manufacturers are no longer required to list the ash content of their food. If you have to guess, know that ash content in canned food is usually under 2%. Between this omission and manufacturers being allowed to use minimums and maximums, instead of actual amounts, these calculations are, by their nature, inaccurate. To get more accurate numbers, you need to call the manufacturer and ask for the ‘typical nutrient analysis’ (TNA) of the food. The Cat Food Composition chart at CatInfo.org is an excellent resource since it was compiled after making many phone calls to get this information for the foods listed.
